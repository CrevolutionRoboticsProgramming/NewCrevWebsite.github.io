2023: MaysOverTone


Quick Facts:

  • Weight: 106lbs
  • Size: 34" L x 26" W x 31" H
  • Top speed: 16.5 ft/s
  • Drive train: MK4i Swerve Drive
  • Status: Disassembled (Frame on Display)

Other Information

  • Intake that can pick up cones and cubes in any orientation.
  • The intake can also shoot cubes to the different levels.
  • As a very low center of gravity to reduce the chance of tipping over and aid in balancing.
  • Fast elevator with a claw to drop the cones on to the nodes at all the levels.

2022: Daedalus


Quick Facts:

  • Weight: 123lbs
  • Size: 28" L x 30" W x 34" H
  • Top speed: 7.1 and 11.7 ft/s
  • Drive train: West Coast ball shifting drive
  • Status: In Service

Other Information

  • Full width intake allowed easy intake of cargo.
  • Uses photonvision to line up the robot to shoot in the upper hub.
  • As a very low center of gravity to reduce the chance of tipping over and aid in climbing.
  • Fast climber to reach the highest bar in under 13 seconds.

2020-2021: Aegis


Quick Facts:

  • Weight: 125lbs
  • Size: 29" L x 29" W x 28" H
  • Top speed: 6 and 13 ft/s
  • Drive train: West Coast ball shifting drive
  • Status: Disassembled

Other Information

  • Not fully completed due to covid.
  • The intake was a fully width intake to help in the pick up of power cells.
  • As a very low center of gravity and height to reduce the chance of tipping over and to fit under the tunnel.
  • A turret shooter to line up with the with the higher power port with increased accuracy.

2019: Asteria


Quick Facts:

  • Weight: 125lbs
  • Size: 33" L x 26" W x 48" H
  • Top speed: 7 and 15 ft/s
  • Drive train: Colson West Coast ball shifting drive
  • Status: In Service

Other Information

  • The drivetrain is 8 wheels drive with 2 speed gearboxes power by 6 mini-CIMS.
  • The gorilla arm able to lift the robot to a level 2 in under 3 seconds.
  • Hatching mechanism able to grab and place the hatch panels fast and reliably.
  • Fast elevator able to place hatch panels and cargo at all levels with speed.
